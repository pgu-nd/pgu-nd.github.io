Biography

I am currently a 5th year Ph.D. student at the University of Notre Dame in the Computer Science and Engineering department. My doctoral advisors are Dr. Danny Z. Chen and Dr. Chaoli Wang. Previously, I received a M.S. in Computer Science and a M.S. in Mathematics from The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley.

My research focuses on the intersection of medical image analysis, scientific visualization and deep learning.
